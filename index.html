Frequently
Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions with answers to clarify doubts.

What is the tattoo process like?

Our tattoo process begins with a consultation to discuss your design ideas, placement, and size. We then create a custom design based on your input. On the day of your appointment, we'll prepare the area, transfer the design, and begin tattooing. Throughout the process, we prioritize your comfort and ensure a sterile environment.

How do I care for my new tattoo?

After getting your tattoo, keep it covered for the first few hours. Gently wash with mild soap, pat dry, and apply a thin layer of recommended ointment. Avoid direct sunlight, swimming, and soaking for at least 2 weeks. Don't pick at scabs or scratch the area. Follow our detailed aftercare instructions for best results.

Can I get a tattoo over a scar?

Yes, in many cases tattoos can be done over scars. However, it depends on the scar's age, type, and condition. Scars should be at least 1-2 years old and fully healed. Our artists will assess your specific situation during a consultation and recommend the best approach for your scar coverage tattoo.

How much does a tattoo cost?

Tattoo pricing varies based on size, detail, placement, and the artist's experience. Small tattoos typically range from ₹1,000-₹3,000, medium tattoos from ₹4,000-₹8,000 per hour, and large pieces can cost ₹24,000-₹1,80,000 for full sessions. We provide detailed pricing information in our pricing section above.

Do tattoos hurt?

Pain levels vary from person to person and depend on the tattoo's location. Areas with thin skin and less fat (like ribs, spine, ankles) tend to be more sensitive. Most clients describe the sensation as uncomfortable but manageable. Our artists work to make the experience as comfortable as possible.
